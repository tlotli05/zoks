Our Story

From a local gathering spot to Mmametlhake's premier nightlife destination, discover the journey of Zodwana Pub & Grill.

From Humble Beginnings

Zodwana Pub & Grill started as a small local spot in Mmametlhake, Mpumalanga, with a simple vision: to create a space where the community could gather, enjoy good food, and experience authentic South African hospitality.

What began as a modest establishment has grown into the premier nightlife destination in the region, known for its electric atmosphere, premium offerings, and unforgettable events.

The name "Zodwana 2.0" represents our evolution - maintaining our roots while embracing modern nightlife culture, creating a unique fusion of traditional South African warmth with contemporary urban energy.

Today, we're more than just a pub - we're a cultural hub where memories are made, friendships are forged, and the spirit of Mmametlhake comes alive every night.

Our Vision & Values

What drives us to create unforgettable experiences

Authentic Experience

We believe in keeping it real - from our traditional street food recipes to our local music selection, every element is authentically South African.

Community First

Zodwana is built by the community, for the community. We prioritize local talent, suppliers, and creating opportunities for Mmametlhake residents.

Premium Quality

While we keep our roots authentic, we never compromise on quality. From our drinks selection to our ingredients, only the best will do.

Rooted in Mmametlhake

Our connection to Mmametlhake runs deep. We're not just located here - we're part of the community's fabric, contributing to local economy and culture.

We source our ingredients locally whenever possible, support local musicians and DJs, and create employment opportunities for community members.

Zodwana Pub & Grill has become a landmark destination that puts Mmametlhake on the map for nightlife and entertainment in Mpumalanga.

We take pride in showcasing the best of what our community has to offer, creating a space that locals are proud to call their own.
